In Seattle

Space Needle

The Space Needle is an observation tower in Seattle, Washington, a landmark of the Pacific Northwest, and an icon of Seattle.

Fremont Troll

The Fremont Troll is a public sculpture in the Fremont neighborhood of Seattle, located under the George Washington Memorial Bridge.

Pike Place Market

Pike Place Market is a public market overlooking the Elliott Bay waterfront in Seattle and is one of the oldest farmers' markets in the US.

Gas Works Park

Gas Works Park in Seattle, Washington is a 19.1-acre public park on the site of the former Seattle Gas Light Company gasification plant.
